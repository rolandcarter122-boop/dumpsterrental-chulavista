Frequently Asked Questions

What dumpster sizes are available?

We offer a range from 10-yard to 40-yard roll off dumpsters depending on your project’s needs.

How much does it cost to rent a dumpster?

Prices vary by size, material type, and rental duration, but we always provide upfront quotes with no surprise charges.

Do I need a permit for a dumpster?

If the dumpster sits on a public street, most cities require a short-term permit. We’ll guide you through that process.

How long can I keep a dumpster rental?

Most customers keep it for 5–7 days, but we offer flexible rental periods for long-term projects.

What items are not allowed in dumpsters?

Hazardous materials, paints, chemicals, and batteries are prohibited by local and federal disposal laws.

Hawaiian Beaches, HI Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Hawaiian Beaches is located on the east side of the island of Hawaii at 19°32′57″N 154°54′27″W (19.549247, -154.907587). It is bordered to the north by Hawaiian Paradise Park, to the south by Pahoa and Nanawale Estates, and to the east by the Pacific Ocean. Hawaii Route 130 forms the western border of the community and leads north 18 miles (29 km) to Hilo.
